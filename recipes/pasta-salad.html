Pasta-salad

Description

A light and fresh pasta salad with cherry tomatoes, arugula, and a simple Italian dressing.

Ingredients

  • 500g of pasta
  • 250g of cherry tomatoes
  • 250g of sieved tomatoes
  • 400g of arugula
  • 5 Tbsp of balsamic vinegar and olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta and strain it
  2. Cut up your tomatoes and put them in the bowl
  3. Wash the Arugula and put it in the bowl
  4. Mix your dressing out of the sieved tomatoes, balsamic vinegar and oil
  5. Add your Pasta into the bowl and mix it
  6. Add the Dressing and enjoy!
