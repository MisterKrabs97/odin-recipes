Bolognese

Description

A classic meat sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, and herbsâ€”perfect for serving over pasta.

Ingredients

  • 500g of ground beef
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • Herbs and spices to your liking
  • 200ml of chicken stock

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef until evenly brown
  2. Add the tomatoes, herbs, spices and chicken stock
  3. Let it simmer until the desired thickness is achieved
