Pizza

Description

A simple homemade pizza recipe with a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, melty cheese, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • A Pizza kit (including sauce)
  • A bag of your favorite shredded cheese
  • Your favorite topping (e.g Tuna, Onions et.)

Steps

  1. Open the Pizza kit and roll out your dough on a baking sheet
  2. Open the included Sauce and apply it evenly
  3. Put your toppings and cheese on the pizza
  4. Bake the pizza in the oven at 180Â°C for 14 minutes.
